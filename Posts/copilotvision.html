Is Copilot Vision Legit?

A short, hands-on test of Copilot Vision to see whether it meaningfully helps real work. Short answer: not yet.

I ran the tool through real workflows to judge usefulness and reliability. The interface feels polished at a glance, but results were inconsistent and often missed important context. Audio and narration quality were noticeably poor in my test, and some outputs were just confusing.

Conclusion: promising idea, but not yet reliable for serious workflows. When it consistently understands context and provides accurate, actionable results, I will update this post.
