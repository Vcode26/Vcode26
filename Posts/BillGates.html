Bill Gates

Bill Gates had a very competitive and rebellious childhood. He was born on Oct. 28, 1955. At Lakeside School, he did many rebellious things, like jailbreaking the school computer to gain free computer time.

Bill had a very complex life at Harvard University. He played late-night poker in his dorm, Currier House. He met Steve Ballmer and became close friends. He enrolled in Harvard’s Math 55, where he learned humility by not being the top student.

In 1975, he dropped out of Harvard and co-founded Microsoft with Paul Allen. In 1985, he launched Windows. In 2000, he stepped down as CEO but remains Co‑Executive Chairman.

I chose Bill Gates because he inspired me to create TBC and to do charity work inside TBC.
